This map take place in a cargo base, and it is swarmed with monsters, mostly zombies, imps and demons.

Easy map, all you have to do is to kill everything with the SSG. First get the yellow key. And then go up the top 
port, kill the 4 revenants with rocket launcher, get the blue key, kill the spectres and demons. After that, press 
a button to open the door, kill 3 hell knights and lower the exit pillar by pressing a switch. And finally, kill 
the cacodemons and exit! After that it's done!

This map is easy, it should be done below 4 minutes.
